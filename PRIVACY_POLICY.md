# Privacy Policy for Mini GTA

**Last Updated:** May 21, 2025

## Overview

Mini GTA ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our data practices.

## Data Collection

**Mini GTA does NOT collect, track, or transmit personal user data.**

## Local Storage

- Game progress and high scores are stored **locally on your device only**
- No data is uploaded to external servers
- All saves remain under your control

## Permissions

The App requests the following permissions:

| Permission | Purpose |
|-----------|---------|
| **INTERNET** | Reserved for future features |
| **ACCELEROMETER** | Device orientation detection |
| **STORAGE** | For game data backup (local only) |

## Third-Party Services

Mini GTA **does not integrate** with:
- Analytics services
- Advertising networks
- Social media platforms
- Cloud services

## User Rights

You have the right to:
- ✅ Delete all game data by uninstalling the app
- ✅ Access your local game files
- ✅ Use the app completely offline

## Security

- No personal information is collected
- No passwords or credentials required
- All data remains on-device

## Children's Privacy

Mini GTA does not:
- Collect information from children
- Track or profile users
- Display targeted advertising
