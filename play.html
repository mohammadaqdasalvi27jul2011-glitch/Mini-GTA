🎮 MINI GTA

4K Edition - Open World Action Game
A fully-featured GTA-style open world action game built with Pygame. Experience dynamic police chases, complete missions, drive vehicles, and build your crime empire!

🌍 Open World

Explore a massive 3000×2000 pixel world with buildings, roads, and vehicles

🔫 Combat System

3 weapons: Pistol, Rifle, Shotgun with different damage and fire rates

🚗 Vehicles

Drive cars, trucks, and motorcycles with unique speed and durability

👮 Police Chase

5-star wanted system with dynamic police AI and combat

🎯 Missions

3 random missions: Kill targets, earn money, reach wanted levels

📊 Progress Tracking

High score system, accuracy tracking, and comprehensive statistics

⌨️ Controls

  • WASD / Arrow Keys - Move or drive
  • Mouse Click - Shoot
  • 1, 2, 3 - Switch weapons
  • E - Enter/Exit vehicle
  • ESC - Pause/Menu
  • SPACE - Start Game
⚠️ How to Play: This is a Python-based game. To play, you need to clone the repository and run it locally on your computer.
